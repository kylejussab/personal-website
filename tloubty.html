Narrative Overview

The Last of Us: Between The Years takes place in the 20 years following Sarah's death and before the main events of the first game. It delves into Joel's character during this period and his grieving process. Explore the relationships Joel forms during this time and uncover some unexpected secrets hidden within the universe of The Last of Us. This game does not contain any spoilers for the main storyline of Part 1 or Part 2.

CREATION

Moodboard

The game needed to feel like The Last of Us, even without saying the name. Familiar characters like Joel and Tess were key, but translating them into a GameBoy style bitmap was what really sold the connection. This is a unique experience set in new locations like Los Angeles with the moodboard reflecting that mix of the new and familiar and it's that crossover that makes this game its own both narratively and stylistically.
